Ajira Chronicles - Writing the Kirinyaga Story

Writing the Kirinyaga Story

I first heard about Ajira digital in 2017 while on an internship with Kirinyaga County Government. I immediately enrolled for the first training lot and got an opportunity to attend a one-week training at KEMI, Nairobi in June 2017. My name is Mbau Patrick Kimenju from Kirinyaga County.

The training exposed me to many areas of online work, and I got mostly interested in Article Writing and Digital Marketing. The Ajira one-month mentorship program then helped me grow and learn on- job. Since then, online work has become my full-time job. I have so far trained over 30 youths and mentored many others on article writing and general online work. I look forward to having a hub at Kerugoya town where I will provide free training and job opportunities to Kirinyaga youths on online work.

Currently, I specialize in SEO articles and design professional business articles through Ergasia Online Company Limited, a company I registered after completing Ajira Digital Business Case study program in 2018. I have grown from working on article writing accounts to working with direct business clients for web design and article writing services. I work with a team of dedicated article writers who have found a full- time job through the article writing business. I am also working with the Judicial transcription team to help reduce judicial transcription backlogs.

I am deeply indebted by Ajira Program for raising me to an iconic article writer and giving me a career, I hadn't imagined of.
