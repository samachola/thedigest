Ajira Chronicles - Youtube University

Youtube University

Kamal ibrahim is a youth leader hailing from Wabera ward Isiolo County. A volunteer at isiolo youth innovation center, an altruist and a pro social member of the community, he is part of the youths who conceptualized the idea of the center in 2018. During the period when COVID-19 forced the entire world to adhere to some restrictions to curb its spread globally, he took the initiative of teaching himself how to be a filmmaker.

Kamal utilized the free WIFI at the center and taught himself how to be a videographer, after acquiring the skills at the center through YouTube online tutorials he started a program dubbed mushenee and soja on YouTube (da prince onit) for his fellow volunteers as part of implementing the creative art department. His future plan is to set up a world class video and audio recording studio to support vulnerable youths who struggle with drug addictions and easy target to be radicalized.
